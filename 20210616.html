Ah, the joys of being a morning person.
I awake to the first rays of sun.
I quickly close my eyes to meditate on life.
When my alarm sounds again two hours later I hit snooze over and over for another hour.
And then I seize the day (unless I don't need to be up for any particular reason, in which case who really can say what happens?).

But I have only been a morning person for a few days now, and in short time my psyche will surely be reforged awake and alert at dawn's earliest hour. Maybe if the coyotes by the pond did not yip and yap so loud at night's darkest hour then all would be perfect.
Alas.
