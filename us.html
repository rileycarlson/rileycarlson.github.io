Us

if i must choose pronouns i choose we us and our
so i may be with you whoever you are
however we feel
whatever we face
whenever the time
wherever the place
we stand not alone but together as one
the world changes around us
it changes us too
it pulls us and twists us and ties us in knots
it turns us against us and darkens our thoughts until
we forget
we are always one
we fight kick yell spit
we angrily shun
we placed here together
we soul mates eternal
we cannot escape until heaven infernal
here is our garden together in strife
we the people are shackled in life
