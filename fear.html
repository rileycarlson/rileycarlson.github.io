Fear

endless unknown
darkness and doubt
worried thought of being without
frightened potential
all gone wrong
lost in depths of vast beyond
blind and mute
frozen in time
stunted and stilted
murder
rewind
I see the world end
I am to blame
I bring death sorrow pain
I see love lost
I see feelings forlorn
I bring lonely tattered torn
I see ugly old uncool
I return as ravening ghoul
I fall and I fail and I fall and I fail
I run hide lock up my heart why fight for this world
I am not what you hope
I will not even try
I can never become
I should not
go on
