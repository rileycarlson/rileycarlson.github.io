Fear

Fear is the unknown
the darkness the doubt
the worried thought of living without
the frightened potential of all gone wrong
the dreadful depths of the vast beyond
it blinds and it mutes and it holds us in line
it stunts and it stilts and it murders our minds
what if the world ends
what if I am to blame
what if death and sorrow and pain
what if love lost and feelings forlorn
what if lonely and tattered and torn
what if ugly and old and uncool
what if I return as a ravening ghoul
what if I fall and I fail and I fall
I should not
I can not
I will not go on
the lights are all out
my time is not long
I will not
I can not
I should not
go on
