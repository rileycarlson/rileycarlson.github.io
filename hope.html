Hope

a light in the dark
just a pinprick I spy
a whisper in answer to thundering 'WHY'
a friend in the crowd
a lingering smile
a cozy place to lay down for a while
the teensy idea that things may go well
the will to get up from the spot where I fell
I rise from the ground
I take one more step
What happened is past
I feel not regret
I learnt from mistakes
I grieved the old pains
Yet I have risen
Again and again
What comes next is not yet
It will be in due time
I may only prepare
be ready to shine
I have only this moment
One instant to be
I have this moment
I have all I need
